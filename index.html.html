Our Story

Born from a passion for authentic Mughlai cuisine, Aligarh Bites brings the royal flavors of Aligarh's legendary kitchens to your plate. Our founder, inspired by generations of family recipes, set out to create a space where tradition meets comfort.

We use only the freshest ingredients and time-honored techniques to craft our dishes. From the slow-cooked korma to the fragrant biryani, every bite is a journey to the heart of Aligarh.
